🚴‍♂️ Bike Store Sales Dashboard – Data Story
📌 Project Overview
This Tableau dashboard analyzes bike store sales performance across product categories, regions, time, and customer demographics. The goal is to uncover key revenue drivers, customer patterns, and business opportunities to support data-driven decision-making.

📖 Business Story
1. Revenue Growth Over Time
The business experienced significant growth starting in 2013, where revenue jumped from ~2.6K to over 24K, and peaked near 29K in 2014 and 2016.
👉 Insight:
This indicates a turning point—possibly due to improved marketing, product expansion, or increased demand. However, the slight dip in 2015 suggests seasonal or operational fluctuations.

2. Product Performance – What Drives Revenue?
Bikes dominate revenue, especially:
Road Bikes (highest avg revenue)
Mountain Bikes
Accessories and Clothing contribute less but still add value.
👉 Insight:
The business is heavily dependent on high-value bike sales, while accessories remain under-leveraged upsell opportunities.

4. Regional Insights – Where Revenue Comes From
United States leads significantly (~27.9M)
Followed by:
Australia (~21.3M)
UK (~10.6M)
Canada (~7.9M)
👉 Insight:
The company has a strong presence in North America, but international markets like Europe show growth potential.

5. Category Contribution by Region
Across all regions:
Bikes generate the highest revenue
Accessories are secondary
Clothing contributes the least
👉 Insight:
The business model is product-centric (bikes) rather than diversified. There is an opportunity to:
Increase bundle sales
Promote accessories and apparel

6. Customer Demographics – Who Buys the Most?
Majority of customers fall between ages 25–45
Peak purchasing group: 30–40 years
👉 Insight:
The core audience is working professionals and active adults, suggesting:
Targeted marketing campaigns
Lifestyle-based promotions (fitness, commuting, adventure)

🔍 Key Takeaways
🚀 Rapid growth phase started in 2013
💰 Bikes are the primary revenue driver
🌍 U.S. is the strongest market
📦 Accessories & clothing are underutilized
👥 Core customers are aged 25–45

💡 Business Recommendations
Increase cross-selling (bike + accessories bundles)
Expand marketing in high-growth international markets
Launch targeted campaigns for 30–40 age group
Analyze 2015 dip to reduce future fluctuations
Promote subscription/service models (maintenance, gear)
